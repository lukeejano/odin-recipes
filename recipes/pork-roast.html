Marinated Pork Roast

This is a great marinade for any meat. I especially like it on pork roasts, but it can easily be
used on chicken or ribs. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) pork roast
  • Â½ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoon cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
  • 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 clover garlic (minced)

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Prepare grill for indirect heat.
  2. Step 2
    In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the Worcestershire sauce, honey, vinegar,
    mustard seed, mustard powder, lemon pepper, celery salt, and garlic; seal, and mix
    ingredients. Place the roast in the plastic bag, press air out of bag, and seal. Marinate
    for 2 hours in the refrigerator, turning the roast occasionally to help coat while marinating.
  3. Step 3
    Lightly oil grill grate. Place roast on grill, and discard marinade. Cover, and cook for
    2 1/2 to 3 hours, or until internal temperature is 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
